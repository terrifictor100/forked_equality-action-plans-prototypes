Action plans

From 2027 onwards, employers with over 250 employees must submit an action plan alongside their gender pay gap report. This action plan must show how they intend to address their gender pay gap, and to support employees going through the menopause.

The actions on your plan must be selected from our list of actions, all of which are backed by evidence demonstrating their effectiveness.

You can use this action shortlist tool to tell us about your organisation and workforce and get tailored recommendations for actions to put on your action plan.
