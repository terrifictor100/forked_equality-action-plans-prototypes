A diversity lead is a role within an organisation. It may be a full time position or a set of responsibilities which can be combined with another role, such as managing director or head of HR. Diversity leads work to improve representation and inclusion of members of minority groups, or staff who are underrepresented in the organisation.

A diversity task force is a diverse collection of people. They have different backgrounds and characteristics, and represent the full diversity of employees in an organisation. A task force should be a way for underrepresented groups to feel heard and for their perspectives to be incorporated into decision-making.
