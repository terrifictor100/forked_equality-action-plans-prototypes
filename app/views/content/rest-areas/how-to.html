There are a number of ways to support employees experiencing symptoms of menopause. Managers can:

  • provide regular training, and facilitate discussions and actions at all levels about equality, diversity and inclusion and the menopause
  • move away from presenteeism as a performance metric and introduce greater flexibility in where and when work is done
  • clearly communicate inclusive policies, such as menopause support and workplace adjustments

  • facilitate more breaks, for example between meetings

Organisations can help lessen the impact of menopause on women at work. They can:

  • provide rest areas and quiet rooms
  • introduce cooling systems or fans for women experiencing hot flushes
  • relax uniform policies
  • provide cooler clothing
  • promote flexibility of location
  • vary shift patterns
