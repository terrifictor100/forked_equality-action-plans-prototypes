An estimated 400,000 women enter menopause each year, typically between the ages of 45 and 55.

Research shows that 67% of working women between the ages of 40 and 60 with experience of menopausal symptoms said they have had a mostly negative impact on them at work.

90% of UK workplaces have no formal support for women experiencing menopause symptoms.

87% of working women want their employers to be more supportive of the challenges of menopause.
