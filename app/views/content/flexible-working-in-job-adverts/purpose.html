Making flexible working more widely available has the potential to normalise it for both women and men. Mentioning flexibility in job adverts should encourage more applicants — and in particular more women — to apply.

By flexible working we mean all types of flexibility. This could mean:

  • the amount of hours worked — for example, part-time
  • the working hours — for example, flexitime or compressed hours
  • the location of the work — for example, working from home or working remotely
  • other arrangements, such as job sharing
