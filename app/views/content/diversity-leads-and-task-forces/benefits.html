Diversity leads and task forces highlight and promote the value of diversity in the workplace.

More generally, benefits of diversity and inclusion are found to include:

  • reduced costs
  • improved resourcing of talented personnel
  • better products and services
  • enhanced corporate image
  • improved creativity and problem-solving
  • better decision making
  • innovation
  • greater flexibility and increased productivity
  • improved organisational performance and efficiency
  • enhanced trust in relationships, satisfaction and commitment within the workforce
  • improved customer relations and service delivery
