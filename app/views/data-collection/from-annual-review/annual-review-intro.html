Action plan annual review

This page would likely only be shown the first time an org needs to do an annual review. Otherwise it would be skipped.

Every year after your action plan is created, you will be asked to review it.

Every three years, this will be a thorough evaluation of all actions and a reselection of new actions for your action plan. In the intervening years, only a brief summary of progress and measurement is required.

As this is your first year of review, we will only ask you two brief summary questions. You can also update your plan at this stage but it is not required.
