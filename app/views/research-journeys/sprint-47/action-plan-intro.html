Action plans

From 2027 onwards, if you have over 250 employees, you must submit an action plan alongside your gender pay gap report. This action plan must show how you intend to address your gender pay gap, and to support employees going through the menopause. (In future years, you will conduct a review of your existing plan rather than creating a new one from scratch.)

The actions on your plan must be selected from our list of actions, all of which are backed by evidence demonstrating their effectiveness.

If you are not sure which actions to select for your plan, you may want to explore our suggested actions tool (opens in a new window) first and come back later to input your plan on this service.
