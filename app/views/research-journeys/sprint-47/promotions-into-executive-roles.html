Promotion refers to a permanent change when an employee advances or rises to a higher office or rank on an ongoing basis or fixed-term contract and does not return to the previous role.

This includes when employees are promoted from one managerial role to another.

Temporary changes, employees relocating to overseas offices, and those that transfer to a position of equal rank (despite an increase in duties) are not considered promotions.
